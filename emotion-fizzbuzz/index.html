🌍 Know Your Carbon. Own Your Change.

EcoTrack helps you understand how your daily choices impact the planet — and what you can do about it, starting today.


Top 5 Ways to Reduce Carbon

  1. Switch to public transport or carpool daily
  2. Eat more plant-based meals each week
  3. Install LED bulbs and smart energy systems
  4. Reduce, reuse, and recycle consistently
  5. Support renewable energy at home and work

Why Carbon Awareness Matters

  • Global temperatures are rising 1.1°C above pre-industrial levels
  • Individual choices account for ~72% of global emissions
  • Small daily habits multiplied by millions = massive impact
  • Awareness is the first step toward sustainable living
  • Future generations depend on actions we take today
